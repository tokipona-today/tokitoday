Sentences using 'li lon'

practice

Here are some examples of sentences using the word li lon.

Inli toki pona
1. The island is to the south of Japan. ma pi insa telo li lon poka pilin pi nasin suno pi ma Nijon.
2. It doesn’t exist. ona li lon ala.
3. Why are there cats in here? tan seme la soweli tomo li lon ni?
4. The bread’s on the table. pan li lon supa.
5. Where’s Tom’s office? tomo pali pi jan Ton li lon seme?
6. Learning a new language is like meting someone for the first time who always claims you are wrong and worse - has the ability to prove it. sina kama sona e jan la jan ni li toki tawa sina li toki tawa sina li toki tawa sina e ni: sina lon ala. jan ni li ken a lawa e ni: ona li lon. ni li sama kama sona pi toki ante.
7. The apple and the rose are from the same family. kili loje en kasi loje li lon kulupu mama sama.
8. He lives in a cozy little house. ona li lon tomo lili.
9. They’re still inside. tenpo ni la ona li lon insa.
10. Can you tell me where the restroom is? sina ken ala ken toki e ni: tomo telo li lon seme?
11. He is living in Tokyo. tomo ona li lon ma tomo Tokijo.
12. Is it real? ni li lon ala lon?
13. It’s right above you. ona li lon sewi pi lawa sina.
14. Where is the nearest pharmacy? esun pi poka nanpa wan pi ilo pi pona sijelo li lon seme?
15. Where is his family? kulupu mama pi mije ni li lon seme?
16. Nothing would exist without a creator. jan ala li lon e ijo la ijo li lon ala.
17. There’s nothing to be scared of. monsuta li lon ala.
18. It was dark when we reached the hotel. mi kama lon tomo lape pi jan tawa la pimeja li lon.
19. Germany is in the middle of Europe. ma Tosi li lon insa pi ma Elopa.
20. Tom is here. jan Ton li lon ni.
21. If Tom were here what would you say to him? jan Ton li lon ni la sina toki e seme tawa ona?
22. The park was deserted. ma pi kasi suli la jan ala li lon.
23. It’s empty. ijo ala li lon insa ona.
24. The cafe is quiet. Only a few people are about. tomo ni pi telo wawa li kalama ala. jan pi mute lili taso li lon.
