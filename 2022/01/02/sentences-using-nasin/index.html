Sentences using 'nasin'

practice

Here are some examples of sentences using the word nasin.

Inli toki pona
1. Do you know a good way to learn new words? sina sona ala sona e nasin pi kama sona lon nimi sin?
2. Tom is a very religious person. jan Ton li jan pi nasin sewi a.
3. Tom was injured in a traffic accident. jan Ton li pakala lon nasin.
4. How many bridges are there in London? nasin pi noka suli li mute seme lon ma tomo Lanten?
5. Dark as it was we managed to find our way back to our tent. pimeja mute li lon taso mi kama lukin e nasin tomo.
6. How do you spell your last name? nasin seme la jan li wile sitelen e nimi pi kulupu mama sina anu seme?
7. The rules are simple. nasin lawa li pona.
8. Please write in a way that concretely conveys the question. o sitelen lon nasin ni: jan li ken kama sona pona e kon pi wile sona.
9. Why didn’t you just ask someone for directions? tan seme la sina toki ala e wile sona pi nasin pona tawa jan?
10. We’re attorneys. mi mute li jan toki pi nasin lawa.
11. Your plan seems excellent. nasin sina li pona mute tawa mi.
12. How are you going to do that? nasin seme la sina pali e ni?
13. All roads lead to Rome. nasin ali li lawa tawa pini sama.
14. This road is very narrow. nasin ni li lili mute.
15. That’s how it started. tenpo pini la ona li open kepeken nasin ni.
16. How did she do what? nasin seme la ona li pali e seme?
17. I’m assuming you have a plan. mi pilin e ni: sina jo e nasin.
18. If you want to learn Toki Pona using the official book is the best way to do it. sina wile kama sona e toki pona la pu li nasin pona nanpa wan tawa ni.
19. How can you have a laptop but no mobile? nasin seme la sina jo e ilo sona e ilo toki ala?
20. Tom has already explained the rules to me. jan Ton li pana e sona nasin tawa mi.
21. I don’t want to do that the same way Tom did. mi wile ala pali e ni kepeken nasin sama jan Ton.
22. I cannot follow his logic. mi ken ala sona e nasin ona.
23. I didn’t like the way she talked to people. nasin toki jan ona li ike tawa mi.
24. I will abolish capital punishment. tenpo kama la mi weka e nasin pi moli jan.
25. I’ve learned to cook. mi kama sona e nasin seli.
