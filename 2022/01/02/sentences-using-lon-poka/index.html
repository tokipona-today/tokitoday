Inli toki pona
1. In chess the bishop is closer to the queen. musi pi jan lawa ma la jan suli li lon poka pi meli lawa.
2. The restaurant is two hundred meters from my office. tomo moku ni li lon poka pi tomo pali mi.
3. It is abnormal to have the heart on the right side. ni li nasa: jan li jo e pilin lon poka lawa.
4. Behind every great man there’s a great woman. mije li pona mute la meli pi pona mute li lon poka mije.
5. He lives next door to us. tomo ona li lon poka pi tomo mi.
6. Tom’s desk is next to Mary’s. supa pali pi jan Ton li lon poka pi supa pali pi jan Mewi.
7. Tom wants to be with me. jan Ton li wile lon poka mi.
8. Tom lives near a river. jan Ton li awen lon poka pi nasin telo.
9. Laika orbited Earth for seven days. soweli tomo Laka li tawa lon poka sike ma lon tenpo suno luka tu.
10. Don’t go anywhere without me. o tawa ijo ala lon poka pi mi ala.
11. They got married on the beach. ona tu li kama kulupu mama lon poka telo.
12. Don’t sit here if you’re not going to play poker with us. sina wile ala musi lipu mani lon poka mi la o lon ala ni.
13. Keep to the right. o awen lon poka lawa.
14. In winter I like to stay at home near the fire and listen to the wind blow. tenpo lete la mi awen lon tomo mi. mi lon poka seli. mi kute e kon tawa. ni li pona tawa mi.
15. I was with him. mi lon poka ona.
16. She isn’t married. ona li jo ala e kulupu mama sin lon poka pi jan olin.
17. Are there bears around here? soweli wawa li lon poka anu seme?
18. This restaurant is located on the riverbed. tomo moku ni li lon poka pi telo tawa.
19. The hospital is near here. tomo pi pona sijelo li lon poka ni.
20. In the table the napkins are placed at right of the plates. supa moku la len uta li lon poka lawa pi poki moku.
21. The satellite is now in orbit. tenpo ni la ilo mun tawa li tawa lon poka sike ma.
22. I didn’t want to go swimming with Tom but I did. mi wile ala tawa telo lon poka pi jan Ton. taso mi tawa.
23. He rose from his chair to welcome me. ona li kama sinpin lon poka pi supa monsi ona li toki e ni tawa mi: mi kama pona.
24. The island is to the south of Japan. ma pi insa telo li lon poka pilin pi nasin suno pi ma Nijon.
25. Tom picked up the black knight and moved it across the board. jan Ton li kama jo e soweli tawa pimeja li pana e ona lon poka ante pi supa musi.
