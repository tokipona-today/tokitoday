A board game

blog

Here are the rules of a board game. Obviously, the game is much simpler than it seems. For me, writing these detailed rules was a language exercise. It turned out to be easier than I expected, once the game vocabulary was established. For you, this article can be good [reading and comprehension] practice.

kama musi

mi pali e musi ni lon tenpo wan pi pali ala. tenpo open la, mi wile pali e musi supa pona pi pali kulupu. taso tenpo ni la, mi kama ala jo e nasin musi pona. tan ni la, mi kama wile kepeken e lipu wan pi toki musi. jan pi kama sona mi (jan Ilisi) li pali e lipu ni. lipu ni li musi li pona tawa mi tan ni: ona li toki e nasin pi pali kulupu tan jan pi ma ante. tan toki ni la, nasin musi li kama, sitelen pi supa musi li kama kin kepeken tenpo lili.

supa musi li jo e lupa mute tan ni: open la, jan musi li o pana e ona lon sewi pi ilo sona lipu. tan ni la, ilo sona li sike e nasin sike li pana e ilo tenpo li pana e leko pi nanpa nasa kepeken jan ala. ni li pona mute. taso jan ale ala li jo e ilo sona lipu. tan ni la, mi ante e musi. ni la, jan li ken musi kepeken ala ilo sona lipu.

lipu pi toki musi

toki musi

ma lili tu li kama wan. jan pi ma tu li mama e jan lili mute. tan ni la, jan pi ma lili tu li kama mute mute. jan li pilin e ni: ma lili tu mi li kama lili ete. tan ni la, ona li wile lanpan e ma lili nanpa tu wan. nimi ona li ma pi tenpo lete. tenpo pini la, jan ala li lon ma ni. tenpo suno ale la, jan wawa tu li pana e kili pan e kili lili walo tawa jan sin pi ma pi tenpo lete. taso pali ni li wile e wawa mute tan ni: ma telo lete en nasin telo li lon. jan wawa li wile tawa nasin suli. kin la, ma pi tenpo lete la, tenpo lili luka luka taso li lon tenpo suno. pana kulupu li wile pini lon tenpo suno. ante la, jan wawa li kama moli tan lete. jan mute li kama moli tan wile moku pi kili pan.

lawa tawa pi kiwen musi

jan musi tu o pana kulupu e poki tu wan (kiwen musi). open la ona li lon nimi ‚Äúpan‚Äù anu nimi ‚Äúkili‚Äù tan kulupu ona. lon supa musi la, ma kiwen pi pana kulupu pini li jo e sitelen nimi (linja tu wan li kili pan, kasi kule li kili lili walo). kiwen musi li kama lon ni la, ona li awen lon ni. jan musi ali li jo e kiwen wawa tu tu.

sike musi sin open la, jan musi li pana e leko nanpa. ona li kama jo e nanpa tawa. jan musi li ken kepeken e nanpa ni tan wile ona :

a) ona li ken tawa e kiwen musi (tawa pi ma kiwen wan li esun e nanpa tawa wan).

b) ona li ken sike e nasin sike (wan anu mute) tan nanpa sike 45 kepeken nanpa tawa wan (jan musi o weka e kiwen wawa kin).

c) ona li ken tawa e tomo tawa telo. tawa pi ma kiwen wan li esun e nanpa tawa wan (jan musi li o weka e kiwen wawa kin).

nasin sike (luka wan li lon)

jan musi li jo e kiwen wawa tu tu. ona li wile sike e nasin sike la, ona o weka e kiwen wawa wan (kiwen musi li ken tawa lon ma pimeja taso). kiwen wawa wan li ken sike e nasin sike tan nanpa sike 45 taso. jan musi li weka e kiwen wawa ale la, ona li ken toki e wile kiwen wawa pi jan musi ante.

tomo tawa telo (wan li lon)

jan musi li ken kepeken e tomo tawa telo. kepeken ona li tawa ma kiwen wan la, o weka e kiwen wawa wan.

pini musi

pana kulupu tu li pini lon tenpo musi pi pini ala la, musi li pini pona. jan musi wan li pini ala lon tenpo musi la, jan musi tu li kama anpa.

musi li pini pona la, jan musi li ken musi sin. taso ilo pi tenpo musi li weka e tenpo lili wan tan tenpo musi.

To make it short :

  1. It‚Äôs a cooperative game.
  2. Each player has 3 pawns that has to be brought to 3 delivery points on the other side of the board.
  3. You throw a dice and move your pawns as you wish accordingly to the points of the dice.
  4. If you need to rotate a platform, it takes 1 point and 1 stone (you have 4 at the beginning of the game). Same for the boat, in the middle.
  5. You can‚Äôt pass nor stay on an occupied spot.
  6. The deliveries have to be finished within 10 minutes.
