lesson

2 - KIPISI TU

Now, let’s listen to the 3 reasons why kili pan Juli worked so hard for us…

1 FIRST PART

  1. Listen the full speed audio and read at the same time.
  2. Use the vocabulary to understand the text.
  3. Read the text out loud.
  4. Hide the text and listen to the 75% speed.
  5. Feel proud (for whatever reason).
Kalama sin #1 75% speed 

        

        		

        

            

        

        		





  
    nanpa wan la kalama pana pi mute lili li lon. kalama ni li lon la jan li ken kute e ona li ken kama sona e ijo pona! 
  






Vocabulary






kalama pana
audio material for learning (in this context)




mute lili
few




lon
to exist




ijo pona
something nice





2 SECOND PART
    

  1. Read the vocabulary (out loud)
    2. 

  2. Listen to the 75% speed audio and try to spot these words
    3. 

  3. Read the English translation. Hide it. Then listen the audio.
    4. 

  4. Translate the English back into toki pona.
    5. 

  5. Listen to the full speed audio and enjoy your understanding.
    6. 







Kalama sin #1
75% speed






        

            

        

        		

        

            

        

        		





  
    nanpa tu la kalama ni li ken pona tawa jan sin pi toki pona. kalama li ken pona e ken kute ona! kalama li ken pona tawa nasin toki ona! ona li kute e ni la ona li ken kama sona e nasin ante mute pi toki pona a. ona li kama sona la kulupu pi toki pona li kama suli li kama pona 
  






Vocabulary






pona
improve




jan sin
newcomers (us!)




ken kute
listening skills




nasin toki
grammar




nasin ante
other ways





3 THIRD PART
    

  1. Listen to the audio (yep, you can frown!).
    2. 

  2. Listen again 3 times. Each time, read one of the propositions below.
    3. 

  3. Pick the one.
    4. 

  4. Translate properly.
    5. 







Kalama sin #1
75% speed






        

            

        

        		

        

            

        

        		





  
    ante la jan li wile toki e ijo pona tawa kulupu la ona li ken toki tawa mi li ken lon kalama nanpa kama!

    2. 

  2. Therefore, if anyone has something to say, they can say it on Discord…
    3. 

  3. Also, if anyone has something to say, it can be said in a next episode
    4.

5 PALI SONA
    

  1. Take your time and try to translate these sentences.
    Remember that you can split ideas in as many segments as you wish. Toki pona likes small statements.
    2. 





a. Also, if anyone wants to complain, they can talk to me
b. You could learn some other ways to cook.
c. Begginers can watch television to improve their listening skills.
d. There is no many people as good as me in the whole world.
e. This podcast is important to me for several reasons.





6 LUKIN SONA
    

  1. Here you have the text that you’ve just studied from the podcast. You will read it at the average same speed as the original audio one. Watch it several times and focus on understanding the more you can. You will quickly be able to catch everything. When you are done, go back to the audio and enjoy!
    2. 

  2. Watch it again and read it aloud, just like if you were recording the podcast yourself.
